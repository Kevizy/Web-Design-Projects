THE BEGINNING OF A NEW ERA!

Too Many Advertisements


Does it feel like you're subjected to advertisements all day? Have you ever had a conversation about something and then start seeing advertisements for that item in your social media feed? A simple google search has ads. Some ads may interest you, while others may not, but there is a lot of discussion nowadays about advertisements and privacy. As more and more people become connected online, and the Internet of Things grows, the number of advertisements will grow as well.


How ad blocking is supposed to help


Whether streaming videos, browsing social media, or just surfing the internet advertisements are everywhere. Many internet browsers automatically block pop-up ads, but almost every website on the internet has banner ads which can't be blocked so easily. Usually installed as a browser extension, ad blocking software filters certain media types and content. Check out our Ad Blocking Information Page for more information.
